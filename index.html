Nikolas Tesla
Resume

He was born in Smilio in the Lika region of today's Croatia, which belonged to the Serbian community of the Austrian Empire. His father was the orthodox priest of the village of Smilian , Milutin Tesla , his mother was Georgina-Džuka Madić , daughter of a priest, while her brothers were also members of the country's clergy . One was Metropolitan Nikola Madić and the other monk Petar Madić. The name Tesla denotes the small ax with a blade at right angles to the handle, however, it is used to describe a person with protruding teeth, a common characteristic of members of the Tesla family
